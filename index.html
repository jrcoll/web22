JOJO'S BIZARRE ADVENTURE

Multimedia Property

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure began as a Japanese manga in the late 1980's but has grown into a multimedia property spanning anime, live-action films, video games, and more. The story follows the Joestar family as they battle evil across generations.


DARK SOULS

Video Game Series

Darks Souls, a third-person action game taking place in a medieval fantasy setting is one of the flagship titles from developer, From Software. Known for it's crushing difficulty and obtuse story-telling I hold this series near and dear to my heart.


PERSONA 5 ROYAL

Video Game

Persona 5 Royal, a video game developed by Atlus, takes place in modern day Tokyo. The story follows highschool students thwarting the cruel plans of evil adults with the power of demons. Think Pokémon, but more evil.
